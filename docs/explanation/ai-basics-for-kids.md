# What is AI? (For Kids!)

Imagine you have a robot friend. At first, the robot doesn't know anything. It doesn't know what a cat is, or how to tell a joke. **Artificial Intelligence (AI)** is like a "learning brain" we give to that robot.

## How does it learn?

Think about how you learned what an apple is. Your parents probably showed you an apple and said, "This is an apple." Then they showed you a banana and said, "This is NOT an apple." 

AI learns the same way! We show it thousands of pictures:
- "This is a cat."
- "This is a dog."
- "This is a toaster."

After seeing enough pictures, the AI starts to notice patterns. It learns that cats have pointy ears and whiskers. It doesn't need to be told exactly what a cat is anymore—it just *knows* because it has seen so many examples!

## Why is it called "Artificial"?

"Artificial" just means "made by people." It's not a biological brain like yours; it's a brain made of computer code. 

## Fun Fact
AI is already all around you! It's what helps your tablet recognize your face, or how Netflix knows which cartoons you might like to watch next.
